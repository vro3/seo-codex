# AI Amplification Brand

## Carrot Wave Mark
- Built from text carrots (`>` or `^`) forming a waveform.
- Expands visually left to right.
- Symbolizes amplification, motion, and the "V" for Vince/VR Creative Group.

## Identity Themes
- Futuristic
- Clean geometric lines
- Motion as a core motif
- Black with neon accents
- Texture-light, contrast-heavy visuals

## Merch Concepts
- Black hoodies with neon wave print
- Tech sleeves
- Geometric back prints
- Holographic stickers
